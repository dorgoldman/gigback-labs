Israeli NGOs report that the hardest parts of governance and ניהול תקין are keeping up with detailed annual reporting to רשם העמותות ורשות המסים, writing and approving the required board documentation on time, and understanding where their organizational gaps are before a regulator or funder forces the issue. [greeninvoice.co](https://www.greeninvoice.co.il/magazine/%D7%90%D7%99%D7%A9%D7%95%D7%A8-%D7%A0%D7%99%D7%94%D7%95%D7%9C-%D7%AA%D7%A7%D7%99%D7%9F/)

Below is a sourced breakdown focused on small–mid עמותות.

## Annual reporting & ניהול תקין – what’s actually required

### From רשם העמותות / רשות התאגידים

Official guidelines and practitioner summaries converge on a clear annual package that an עמותה must submit to maintain or obtain אישור ניהול תקין: [david-frenkel.co](https://www.david-frenkel.co.il/associations-and-endowments/submission-of-reports-to-the-registrar/)

- **Deadline:**  
  רשם העמותות requires submission of the annual reports “לא יאוחר מיום 30 ביוני בשנה שלאחר שנת הדוח”, with possible extensions via the CPA association; late filing can lead to loss of eligibility for אישור ניהול תקין. [gov](https://www.gov.il/BlobFolder/policy/proper_management_guidelines/ar/association-2018.pdf)

- **Core documents for אישור ניהול תקין:**  
  Practitioners summarize the four key documents: [greeninvoice.co](https://www.greeninvoice.co.il/magazine/%D7%90%D7%99%D7%A9%D7%95%D7%A8-%D7%A0%D7%99%D7%94%D7%95%D7%9C-%D7%AA%D7%A7%D7%99%D7%9F/)
  1. **דוח כספי (כולל מאזן)** — for NGOs with annual turnover above 500,000 ₪ (some sources say 0.5M, others 0.5M–1.25M ₪), audited and signed by two board members; larger NGOs must appoint an external CPA. [haogdan.migzar3.org](https://haogdan.migzar3.org.il/%D7%93%D7%95%D7%97%D7%95%D7%AA_%D7%A9%D7%A0%D7%AA%D7%99%D7%99%D7%9D_(%D7%9B%D7%A1%D7%A4%D7%99%D7%99%D7%9D_%D7%95%D7%9E%D7%99%D7%9C%D7%95%D7%9C%D7%99%D7%99%D7%9D))
  2. **דוח מילולי** — narrative report describing activities, structure, donors, suppliers and foreign‑state donations, also signed by two board members; must be submitted even if there was no financial activity. [greeninvoice.co](https://www.greeninvoice.co.il/magazine/%D7%93%D7%95%D7%97-%D7%9E%D7%99%D7%9C%D7%95%D7%9C%D7%99/)
  3. **פרוטוקול אסיפה כללית** — minutes of the general assembly meeting that approved both financial and narrative reports, with a signed form reporting the assembly’s decisions. [npo.co](https://www.npo.co.il/%D7%94%D7%92%D7%A9%D7%AA-%D7%9E%D7%A1%D7%9E%D7%9B%D7%99%D7%9D-%D7%9C%D7%A8%D7%A9%D7%9D-%D7%94%D7%A2%D7%9E%D7%95%D7%AA%D7%95%D7%AA/)
  4. **המלצות ועדת ביקורת / גוף מבקר** — written recommendations on the reports and governance, signed by the audit committee or external auditor. [david-frenkel.co](https://www.david-frenkel.co.il/associations-and-endowments/submission-of-reports-to-the-registrar/)

- **Additional required details inside the reports:**  
  - רשימת חמשת מקבלי השכר הגבוה בעמותה (including salary and benefits). [haogdan.migzar3.org](https://haogdan.migzar3.org.il/%D7%93%D7%95%D7%97%D7%95%D7%AA_%D7%A9%D7%A0%D7%AA%D7%99%D7%99%D7%9D_(%D7%9B%D7%A1%D7%A4%D7%99%D7%99%D7%9D_%D7%95%D7%9E%D7%99%D7%9C%D7%95%D7%9C%D7%99%D7%99%D7%9D))
  - פירוט תרומות מעל סף מסוים (20,000 ₪ or higher, depending on guideline version) and תרומות מישות מדינית זרה, including quarterly reporting on such donations above thresholds. [gov](https://www.gov.il/BlobFolder/policy/proper_management_guidelines/ar/association-2018.pdf)
  - דיווח על תביעות נגד העמותה או חברי ועד, או הצהרה שאין תביעות. [npo.co](https://www.npo.co.il/%D7%94%D7%92%D7%A9%D7%AA-%D7%9E%D7%A1%D7%9E%D7%9B%D7%99%D7%9D-%D7%9C%D7%A8%D7%A9%D7%9D-%D7%94%D7%A2%D7%9E%D7%95%D7%AA%D7%95%D7%AA/)

- **Eligibility criteria beyond documents:**  
  GreenInvoice’s 2025 guide notes that to be eligible for אישור ניהול תקין the עמותה must: [greeninvoice.co](https://www.greeninvoice.co.il/magazine/%D7%90%D7%99%D7%A9%D7%95%D7%A8-%D7%A0%D7%99%D7%94%D7%95%D7%9C-%D7%AA%D7%A7%D7%99%D7%9F/)
  - Have at least **two years of continuous activity**.  
  - Have no significant management irregularities (“לא התגלו ליקויים בניהול השוטף”).  
  - Submit all required documents (financial and narrative reports, assembly approvals) on time.  
  - Pay the annual fee and submit the request online.  

There is also a separate “אישור הגשת מסמכים” path for younger NGOs (<2 years), which still requires appointing organs (board, audit committee, CPA) and submitting basic reports, but gives a partial status used for סעיף 46 and state contracts. [greeninvoice.co](https://www.greeninvoice.co.il/magazine/%D7%90%D7%99%D7%A9%D7%95%D7%A8-%D7%A0%D7%99%D7%94%D7%95%D7%9C-%D7%AA%D7%A7%D7%99%D7%9F/)

### From רשות המסים (מס הכנסה)

For tax compliance, NGOs recognized as מלכ״ר must file annual income‑tax reports, independent of ניהול תקין: [david-frenkel.co](https://www.david-frenkel.co.il/associations-and-endowments/income-tax-reports/)

- **Annual tax report:**  
  - **טופס 1215 – “דוח שנתי על הכנסות והוצאות של מוסד ציבורי”** — for NGOs and public institutions; for tax year 2024, companies and מלכ״רים must submit by 31.7.2025. [gov](https://www.gov.il/he/service/itc1215)
  - Attach audited financial statements (balance sheet, activity statement, cash‑flow, notes) signed by the NGO chair and CPA. [david-frenkel.co](https://www.david-frenkel.co.il/associations-and-endowments/income-tax-reports/)
  - Additional forms: 1216 (assets), 1217א/1217ב (incoming/outgoing donations), and others depending on activity. [gov](https://www.gov.il/he/service/itc1215)

- **Certificates tied to reporting:**  
  NGOs must also maintain **אישור ניהול ספרים** and **אישור ניכוי מס במקור**, renewed annually; these certify that accounting books are properly kept and determine withholding rates; without valid certificates, clients must withhold up to 30% of payments. [jbclock](https://www.jbclock.com/%D7%A0%D7%99%D7%94%D7%95%D7%9C-%D7%A1%D7%A4%D7%A8%D7%99%D7%9D-%D7%95%D7%A0%D7%99%D7%9B%D7%95%D7%99-%D7%9E%D7%A1-%D7%91%D7%9E%D7%A7%D7%95%D7%A8/)

### Failure rates and common mistakes

- In December 2024, רשמת העמותות announced revocation of ניהול תקין approvals for about **200 עמותות וחל״צ** whose two‑year approvals (2024–2025) were conditioned on timely submission of 2023 reports; those organizations failed to meet the reporting obligation, leading to automatic cancellation. [makorrishon.co](https://www.makorrishon.co.il/news/article/302282)
- The notice emphasizes that annual reports are “כלי מרכזי לפיקוח על פעילות העמותות והחל\"צ… באמצעותם נבדקת עמידת הארגונים בהוראות החוק ובמטרותיהם הציבוריות”, underlining that missing or late reports are a primary reason for losing status. [makorrishon.co](https://www.makorrishon.co.il/news/article/302282)

Practitioner guides highlight typical pitfalls: [er-cpa](https://er-cpa.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/%D7%94%D7%A0%D7%97%D7%99%D7%95%D7%AA-%D7%9C%D7%94%D7%92%D7%A9%D7%AA-%D7%94%D7%93%D7%99%D7%95%D7%95%D7%97%D7%99%D7%9D-%D7%94%D7%A9%D7%A0%D7%AA%D7%99%D7%99%D7%9D-%D7%9C%D7%A8%D7%A9%D7%9D-%D7%94%D7%A2%D7%9E%D7%95%D7%AA%D7%95%D7%AA.pdf)

- **Late submission** beyond 30 June without agreed CPA extension — “איחור בהגשה עלול לשלול את זכאות העמותה לקבלת אישור ניהול תקין לאותה שנה”. [gov](https://www.gov.il/BlobFolder/policy/proper_management_guidelines/ar/association-2018.pdf)
- **Incomplete package** — e.g., submitting the financial report but missing the narrative report, assembly protocol or audit committee recommendations, or not listing top salaries and large donors. [david-frenkel.co](https://www.david-frenkel.co.il/associations-and-endowments/submission-of-reports-to-the-registrar/)
- **Contradictions between reports** — consultants warn that inconsistencies between דוח מילולי and דוח כספי (e.g., activities described but not reflected in expenses, or foreign donations not reported in both documents) lead to queries and delays. [abg-group.co](https://abg-group.co.il/report-registrar-nonprofits/)
- **Governance gaps** — missing audit committee, failing to hold and document an annual general assembly, or not updating changes in board membership and signatories; these issues often surface when the registrar reviews the narrative report and attached protocols. [malkar.co](https://www.malkar.co.il/%D7%97%D7%95%D7%A7%D7%99%D7%9D-%D7%95%D7%AA%D7%A7%D7%A0%D7%95%D7%AA/%D7%94%D7%A0%D7%97%D7%99%D7%95%D7%AA-%D7%9C%D7%94%D7%AA%D7%A0%D7%94%D7%9C%D7%95%D7%AA-%D7%A2%D7%9E%D7%95%D7%AA%D7%95%D7%AA/)

For small NGOs, the heavy documentation and exact deadlines—especially around the narrative report and assembly protocol—are repeatedly described as the most confusing and error‑prone parts.

## Board meetings & reporting – what NGOs find painful

### What board meetings typically involve

Law and guidelines require that the general assembly and board approve the annual reports; templates and public NGO protocols show what this looks like: [gov](https://www.gov.il/BlobFolder/policy/cooperative-committee-protocol-example/he/protocol_vaad_aguda.doc)

- The registrar’s guidelines specify that the assembly must approve both reports and that the protocol documenting this decision be signed by at least two board members and attached to the annual submission. [npo.co](https://www.npo.co.il/%D7%94%D7%92%D7%A9%D7%AA-%D7%9E%D7%A1%D7%9E%D7%9B%D7%99%D7%9D-%D7%9C%D7%A8%D7%A9%D7%9D-%D7%94%D7%A2%D7%9E%D7%95%D7%AA%D7%95%D7%AA/)
- Government and NGO templates for board protocols include: date, list of participants and absentees, agenda items, summary of discussions, decisions, voting results and signatures of the chair and protocol writer. [vaadplus.co](https://vaadplus.co.il/blog/protocol-asefat-vaad-bayit-template)
- Published board minutes from NGOs (e.g., choirs, synagogues, community associations) show recurring agenda items: approval of previous minutes, budget and financial updates, project reports, staffing issues and logistics, all needing formal documentation and archiving. [milachoirs](https://www.milachoirs.com/protocols)

### Pain points around board governance

While there is little quantitative data, several Hebrew sources give direct hints at what feels hardest: [bdo.co](https://www.bdo.co.il/he-il/%D7%9B%D7%AA%D7%91%D7%95%D7%AA-%D7%95%D7%9E%D7%90%D7%9E%D7%A8%D7%99%D7%9D/%D7%A1%D7%A7%D7%98%D7%95%D7%A8%D7%99%D7%9D/%D7%94%D7%9E%D7%92%D7%96%D7%A8-%D7%94%D7%A9%D7%9C%D7%99%D7%A9%D7%99-%D7%9E%D7%9C%D7%9B-%D7%A8%D7%99%D7%9D/%D7%9E%D7%95%D7%A1%D7%93%D7%95%D7%AA-%D7%94%D7%A2%D7%9E%D7%95%D7%AA%D7%94-%D7%95%D7%94%D7%97%D7%9C-%D7%A6-2025-%E2%80%93-%D7%94%D7%9E%D7%93%D7%A8%D7%99%D7%9A-%D7%94%D7%9E%D7%A7%D7%99%D7%A3-%D7%9C%D7%AA%D7%A4%D7%A7%D7%99%D7%93%D7%99%D7%9D,-%D7%A1%D7%9E%D7%9B%D7%95%D7%99%D7%95%D7%AA-%D7%95%D7%A1%D7%93%D7%A8%D7%99-%D7%A2%D7%91%D7%95%D7%93%D7%94)

- A transcript from a court‑related document captures the sentiment: “זה מעיק, אתה יושב עם אנשי נודניקי… לדצמבר יש ישיבת ועד מנהל”, illustrating how some board members experience meetings as burdensome and time‑consuming, especially when they are volunteers juggling other roles. [odata.org](https://www.odata.org.il/dataset/79f22362-ca2c-470a-9071-9c2b427c6eed/resource/35845ce3-87d7-46c6-9565-1ad2db264957/download/-17.12.2018-.pdf)
- BDO’s 2025 guide on NGO organs notes that “ניהול עמותה הוא תהליך מורכב שלא מסתכם רק בגיוס משאבים והפעלת תכניות”, and emphasizes that boards must understand roles, responsibilities and work procedures; the complexity of these requirements is one reason NGOs seek external help. [bdo.co](https://www.bdo.co.il/he-il/%D7%9B%D7%AA%D7%91%D7%95%D7%AA-%D7%95%D7%9E%D7%90%D7%9E%D7%A8%D7%99%D7%9D/%D7%A1%D7%A7%D7%98%D7%95%D7%A8%D7%99%D7%9D/%D7%94%D7%9E%D7%92%D7%96%D7%A8-%D7%94%D7%A9%D7%9C%D7%99%D7%A9%D7%99-%D7%9E%D7%9C%D7%9B-%D7%A8%D7%99%D7%9D/%D7%9E%D7%95%D7%A1%D7%93%D7%95%D7%AA-%D7%94%D7%A2%D7%9E%D7%95%D7%AA%D7%94-%D7%95%D7%94%D7%97%D7%9C-%D7%A6-2025-%E2%80%93-%D7%94%D7%9E%D7%93%D7%A8%D7%99%D7%9A-%D7%94%D7%9E%D7%A7%D7%99%D7%A3-%D7%9C%D7%AA%D7%A4%D7%A7%D7%99%D7%93%D7%99%D7%9D,-%D7%A1%D7%9E%D7%9B%D7%95%D7%99%D7%95%D7%AA-%D7%95%D7%A1%D7%93%D7%A8%D7%99-%D7%A2%D7%91%D7%95%D7%93%D7%94)
- Guidance documents on NGO organs stress that the board, audit committee and general assembly must all function and document decisions properly; many small NGOs struggle to maintain this ongoing governance workload on volunteer time. [dialogue-learning.co](https://dialogue-learning.co.il/corporationsSite/files/mosdotInfo.pdf)
- Research on barriers to implementing Monday.com in social organizations points to board‑level dynamics—skepticism, lack of time to learn new tools, and difficulty aligning volunteer board members around structured workflows—as a barrier to adopting better governance systems. [ehudst.co](https://www.ehudst.co.il/post/barriers-monday-implementation-social-orgs-israel)

In practice, the “pain” is not only the meeting itself, but the need to produce legally sound protocols, align them with reporting requirements, and get signatures from volunteers who may be busy or geographically dispersed.

## Gap mapping & organizational health assessments

### Existing frameworks and tools

Israeli NGOs have access to several structured frameworks, mostly via intermediaries rather than self‑service tools: [midot.org](https://midot.org.il/)

- **תו מידות לאפקטיביות (Midot effectiveness seal)**  
  Midot describes its effectiveness seal as evaluating “את סיכוייה של עמותה לייצר ערך חברתי ולהביא לשינוי לטובה בחיי המוטבים שלה, תוך מיצוי מיטבי של משאבים”, using a methodology that examines planning, execution, measurement & learning, leadership and finance. [midot.org](https://midot.org.il/about)
  The process involves several weeks of interviews, document review and analysis, resulting in a numerical score; only NGOs scoring 75+ receive the seal, which is used by donors and platforms such as JGive and עיגול לטובה as a filter. [sfi.org](https://sfi.org.il/projects/effectiveness/)

- **Self‑assessment data tools (Midot / Nova / Code for Israel)**  
  Midot’s “כלי דאטה” include a self‑assessment questionnaire “המבוסס על הפרמטרים המרכזיים במתודולוגיית מידות”, and a collaborative project with Code for Israel to build an automatic digital tool for NGO rating based on open data, aimed at assessing financial resilience and organizational robustness. [midot.org](https://midot.org.il/data-tools)
  The “שער להערכה” initiative by Nova (part of the SFI group that includes Midot) aggregates evaluation studies of social programs, providing a meta‑layer for organizational learning. [midot.org](https://midot.org.il/data-tools)

- **Guides and checklists from JDC/ELKA and “האוגדן”**  
  The “האוגדן” knowledge base for NGOs includes practical guidance on annual financial and narrative reports, listing required components and emphasizing transparency, top‑salary reporting, foreign‑donation disclosures and governance documentation. [haogdan.migzar3.org](https://haogdan.migzar3.org.il/%D7%93%D7%95%D7%97%D7%95%D7%AA_%D7%A9%D7%A0%D7%AA%D7%99%D7%99%D7%9D_(%D7%9B%D7%A1%D7%A4%D7%99%D7%99%D7%9D_%D7%95%D7%9E%D7%99%D7%9C%D7%95%D7%9C%D7%99%D7%99%D7%9D))
  JDC/ELKA and various NGO support sites provide checklists for organs (board, audit committee, internal auditor) and compliance milestones, effectively acting as manual gap‑mapping tools. [malkar.co](https://www.malkar.co.il/%D7%97%D7%95%D7%A7%D7%99%D7%9D-%D7%95%D7%AA%D7%A7%D7%A0%D7%95%D7%AA/%D7%94%D7%A0%D7%97%D7%99%D7%95%D7%AA-%D7%9C%D7%94%D7%AA%D7%A0%D7%94%D7%9C%D7%95%D7%AA-%D7%A2%D7%9E%D7%95%D7%AA%D7%95%D7%AA/)

### How gap mapping is usually done

Most small–mid NGOs appear to rely on consultants rather than automated diagnostics: [tamir-s.co](https://tamir-s.co.il/%D7%92%D7%99%D7%95%D7%A1-%D7%9E%D7%A9%D7%90%D7%91%D7%99%D7%9D-%D7%9C%D7%A2%D7%9E%D7%95%D7%AA%D7%95%D7%AA/%D7%90%D7%99%D7%A9%D7%95%D7%A8-%D7%A0%D7%99%D7%94%D7%95%D7%9C-%D7%AA%D7%A7%D7%99%D7%9F/)

- NGO‑focused service providers describe their work as “ליווי וייעוץ מקצועי… אבחון ארגוני… מיפוי פערים בניהול ובדיווח”, often starting from reviewing past reports, governance documents and compliance history. [amutotcenter.co](https://amutotcenter.co.il/category/%D7%A0%D7%99%D7%94%D7%95%D7%9C-%D7%A2%D7%9E%D7%95%D7%AA%D7%94/)
- Legal and accounting firms emphasize the need for “בדיקה קפדנית של כל הפרטים המפורטים בדוח… ניהול פנקסים מסודרים… עבודה עם גורם מקצועי”, indicating that gap mapping is largely manual: reviewing reports, protocols, and control processes to identify vulnerabilities. [abg-group.co](https://abg-group.co.il/report-registrar-nonprofits/)

From a product perspective, structured, digital “health checks” that combine governance, finance and impact metrics are still limited; Midot’s tools and some donor platforms occupy part of this space, but ordinary NGOs mostly see gap mapping as something a consultant or auditor does once a problem appears.

- “אישור ניהול ספרים – למי זה נדרש ואיך מורידים אונליין?” — often asked by NGOs alongside ניהול תקין because state funding and large donors require both certificates. [numerotech.co](https://numerotech.co.il/tax-certificates/)
- “איך ממלאים דוח מילולי לרשם העמותות בלי שיחזירו אותו עם הערות ותיקונים?” — the GreenInvoice guide explicitly positions itself as answering this concern. [greeninvoice.co](https://www.greeninvoice.co.il/magazine/%D7%93%D7%95%D7%97-%D7%9E%D7%99%D7%9C%D7%95%D7%9C%D7%99/)
- “עמותות והקדשים » הגשת דוח\"ות לרשם – מה צריך להגיש בבקשה לאישור ניהול תקין?” [david-frenkel.co](https://www.david-frenkel.co.il/associations-and-endowments/submission-of-reports-to-the-registrar/)
- “שאלה לגבי חוק חובת המכרזים ותאגידים עירוניים… העמותה העירונית מקבלת מדי שנה מרשם העמותות אישורי ניהול תקין…” — a legal forum question tying ניהול תקין to procurement law. [din.co](https://www.din.co.il/forum13/542958/)
- NGO support sites repeatedly invite questions with phrasing like “ואם כל זה ייראה לכם מסובך וגוזל זמן יקר, נשמח לספק לכם שירותי ייעוץ… או לענות על כל שאלה בפורום שלנו”, indicating that many queries are informal and revolve around confusion and time pressure. [orelinsurance.wixsite](https://orelinsurance.wixsite.com/the-center-of-co/toolbox)

These phrases give you direct language for marketing content and UX copy: people frame their problems around “מהו אישור… איך מקבלים… איך ממלאים… מה צריך להגיש… למה זה חובה”.

## What triggers NGO leaders to seek governance help

Sources aimed at NGO managers and accountants point to a mix of acute incidents and slow‑burn pressures: [amutotcenter.co](https://amutotcenter.co.il/%D7%93%D7%95%D7%97%D7%95%D7%AA-%D7%A9%D7%A0%D7%AA%D7%99%D7%99%D7%9D/)

**Acute triggers:**

- **Loss or threat of loss of ניהול תקין**  
  The registrar’s revocation of approvals for ~200 NGOs in 2024 explicitly ties non‑submission of reports to immediate loss of funding eligibility, which is the kind of shock that pushes directors to call lawyers and CPAs. [makorrishon.co](https://www.makorrishon.co.il/news/article/302282)
  Practitioners warn that “אי הגשה דוחות שנתיים אלו מעכבת את הכנסות ותקציבי העמותה ועשויה להביא להפסקת פעילותה”, so impending loss of grants or state contracts is a strong trigger. [amutotcenter.co](https://amutotcenter.co.il/%D7%93%D7%95%D7%97%D7%95%D7%AA-%D7%A9%D7%A0%D7%AA%D7%99%D7%99%D7%9D/)

- **Funder or governmental audit**  
  Articles on narrative and financial reports emphasize that without proper reporting, NGOs may face “קשיים משפטיים משמעותיים… אי הענקת ‘אישור ניהול תקין’ או תלונות ציבוריות”, and that state funders and major foundations review these reports closely. [hashavia.co](https://www.hashavia.co.il/%D7%93%D7%95%D7%97%D7%95%D7%AA-%D7%9E%D7%A1-%D7%94%D7%9B%D7%A0%D7%A1%D7%94-%D7%95%D7%97%D7%95%D7%91%D7%95%D7%AA-%D7%93%D7%99%D7%95%D7%95%D7%97-%D7%9C%D7%A2%D7%9E%D7%95%D7%AA%D7%95%D7%AA/)
  where NGOs have foreign‑state donations or complex grant streams, fear of an audit on תרומות מישות מדינית זרה or top‑salary disclosure often pushes them to seek professional governance help. [gov](https://www.gov.il/BlobFolder/policy/proper_management_guidelines/ar/association-2018.pdf)

- **Appointment of a חשב מלווה or entry into a “תכנית לתיקון ליקויים”**  
  Registrar guidelines mention programs for correcting deficiencies; being placed in such a program (e.g., due to deficits or repeated reporting issues) is an obvious trigger for external advisory. [library.mevaker.gov](https://library.mevaker.gov.il/sites/DigitalLibrary/Documents/2017-67b-215-Amutot.pdf)

**Gradual triggers:**

- **Growing complexity and size**  
  Guides stress that “ניהול עמותה הוא תהליך מורכב” and that growing budgets, multiple projects and paid staff require more robust governance—prompting NGOs to seek organizational health assessments like Midot’s effectiveness seal. [calcalist.co](https://www.calcalist.co.il/local_news/article/ry5azowmp)
- **Donor expectations and reputational pressure**  
  Midot and donor‑oriented articles highlight that donors and corporate responsibility units increasingly use effectiveness seals and proof of proper management as a precondition for support; NGOs see participation in such assessments as both marketing and governance improvement. [midot.org](https://midot.org.il/requests)

- **Internal awareness of messiness**  
  Consultancy sites repeatedly say “ואם כל זה ייראה לכם מסובך וגוזל זמן יקר…” and offer “אבחון ארגוני” and “מיפוי פערים”; this suggests that many NGO directors come forward when they realize reports, protocols and financials are being managed ad‑hoc and want to pre‑empt future problems. [tamir-s.co](https://tamir-s.co.il/%D7%92%D7%99%D7%95%D7%A1-%D7%9E%D7%A9%D7%90%D7%91%D7%99%D7%9D-%D7%9C%D7%A2%D7%9E%D7%95%D7%AA%D7%95%D7%AA/%D7%90%D7%99%D7%A9%D7%95%D7%A8-%D7%A0%D7%99%D7%94%D7%95%D7%9C-%D7%AA%D7%A7%D7%99%D7%9F/)

For your product strategy, the “moment of truth” is often:
- a looming reporting deadline with messy data and no narrative draft,  
- an urgent grant or government tender requiring proof of ניהול תקין, סעיף 46 and clean governance, or  
- a board or donor pushing for a more formal “health check” (e.g., Midot seal), revealing process, documentation and data gaps.
